Modding for Client and Browser: Step-by-Step Instructions
First, open the assets repository.
Then, use the search bar to find assets like 'wall,' 'floor,' 'hallway,'
'pillar,' 'desert,' 'ground,' or 'grass.'
Once familiar, clear your search and click on 'Select Assets.'
Now, you can click on the cards you wish to change and edit.
You can also search while in select mode!
You have two options: edit an already existing texture,
or create a new one to replace it.
If you choose to create a new one, select its color and resolution.
This action applies the edits uniformly to all selected textures.
When finished, click 'Save New Textures.'
A dialog box will appear, confirming they are saved to memory.
You're now back on the cards page.
You can repeat the process with more texture groups or individuals,
or you can continue if you're done.
With all changes made, click 'Download all as zip.'
Finally, ask yourself: Are you playing the game on a browser,
or on the client?
