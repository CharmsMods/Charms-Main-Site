Okay, so you're on Client!
Once the download process is complete (please be patient!),
go to your Downloads folder.
Extract the new .zip folder you just received.
You'll now have a new folder named "Venge Client."
Take this folder and copy it into your Documents folder.
VERY IMPORTANT: Do not place this in your
OneDrive Documents, as it will not work.
It needs to be in your local Documents.
You should now have a folder named "Venge Client" in your Documents.
Now you're all set!
You can open your client, and the mod should be applied!
Enjoy!
